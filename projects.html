BEAUTIFICATION PROJECT

Upcoming Project: We have undertaken the project of beautifying and fixing the little libraries around Coppell. This project will allow us to make a mark in our community as well as bring these little libraries back to life

FUNDRAISER AT THE COPPELL FARMERS MARKET

Successful fundraiser at the Coppell Farmers Market on June 26 and July 17. We sold our signature handmade bracelets as well as handmade cards and made a total of over $700! This will allow us to fund our future projects that give back to the community.

GOODIE BAGS FOR KIDS

For Halloween, we donated 50+ Halloween themed goodie bags with handmade friendship bracelets, customized cards, and little activities for the kids at Children’s Health Center in Dallas. Halloween is a holiday where many kids go out and have fun. Since the children in the hospital couldn’t get to Trick or Treat, or dress-up, we decided to bring the party to them. We put bubbles and coloring books in the goodie bags, so the kids could play with their family and friends. We also included our signature handmade friendship bracelets and personalized notes to help the kids feel loved and cherished. The humorous personalized cards with unique Halloween puns hopefully put a smile on their faces and offered them unconditional comfort.

CARDS FOR CANCER PATIENTS

We donated 200 cards to the organization, Send a Smile, in efforts of bringing joy and happiness to 200 cancer patients. The package contains 200 handmade customized cards with a Valentine’s Day theme as February is the month of love and affection. We made these cards with time and care and hopefully the recipients enjoyed them.
